FIRE EXTINGUISHERS

Why You Need Them

Fires can break out at anytime at an office, factory, or home. Fire extinguishers are one of the best ways to protect your assets and employees from fires. These are usually placed around a building and used to suppress small fires before they get out of hand. Fire extinguishers are also a lot more cost effective compared to other fire suppression techniques.

Fire extinguishers are used by spraying a fire with chemicals. The chemicals in a fire extinguisher depends on what type of fire they are intended to put out. This can be confusing, so if you are unsure we will help you chose what is best for your business or home.

Fire Extinguisher Inspections

Fire extinguishers don't last forever. Overtime, the pressurized air can leak out or they can go bad. You don't want an extinguisher to go bad and then need to use it. Our professionals will inspect your extinguishers to make sure they are in working order.
