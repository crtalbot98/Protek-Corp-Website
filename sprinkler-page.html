SPRINKLER SYSTEMS

Why You Need Them

Fires are dangerous as they can destroy a building in a relatively short amount of time. A sprinkler system will slow and prevent the spread of a fire inside your company with water.

Sprinkler systems prevent fires from growing and damaging your assets, harming employees, or destroying your property. Sprinkler systems are required for most commercial properties. If you are not sure if your property requires a sprinkler system, then feel free to contact us and we will let you know. Even if you may not need one, a sprinkler system can and will save lives and Protek your assets.

Installations and Inspections

At Protek we will install and inspect a sprinkler system for your business. We will install a system that protects your business and employees. After installation it is important to make sure everything is running correctly. So, we will also inspect your previously installed system to make sure it will Protek your assets.

We will install and inspect any sprinkler system that is within 60 miles of the Richmond, Indiana area.
