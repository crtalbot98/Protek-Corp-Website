ABOUT US

Our Mission

To Protek the businesses, homes and people in and around Richmond, Indiana.

Our Goal

At Protek we strive to install and inspect, sprinkler systems, fire alarms, security alarms, and fire extinguishers. Every installation and inspection is done by professionals and complete with the utmost care. We want every customer to be satisfied with our company and work.

Our Promise

At Protek we promise to care for your assets and company. We will care for you business like it is our while we are installing or inspecting a system. We will do the best work we can and make sure everything in completed to your and tour businesses liking.

Proteking assests since 1984

Protek Company was founded by Raymond Talbot in 1984. Raymond and his wife, Barbara ran and operated a fire protection installation, service and inspection company located in Centerville, Indiana. Servicing Wayne County and the surrounding area.

In 1999 Protek Company became Protek Corporation and relocated to Richmond, Indiana. We incorporated and added commercial fire and security alarms to our service.

In 2007 Protek also added fire extinguisher sales, service, and maintenance. We also purchased a local security alarm company and added residential security alarms, video surveillance and access controls to our services. After the unexpected passing of Ray in 2016 his wife, Barbara, and son, Mikel, who has worked in the family business since 1993. They transitioned to take over Ray's day to day operations to provide and affordable quality service with integrity and full customer satisfaction.
