WHAT WE DO

Protek Corporation installs, services, and maintains security and fire alarms, fire suppression systems, access control systems, and fire extinguishers. Being a locally owned company we are on call 24/7 for all your needs

Whether it be commercial, industrial, or residential your highest priority of protecting your investments becomes ours. Your family, pets, structures, equipment, and employees deserve to know that they can count on you to provide them with the best protection available and we are here to help you make the best and most economical decisions.

WHO WE ARE

Protek Corporation is a fire protection equipment supplier in Richmond, Indiana. We have been installing and inspecting sprinkler systems and alarm systems since 1984. We have been dedicated to supplying and refilling fire extinguishers since 2003. We are committed to providing a reliable and trustworthy service to our customers.

PROTEK YOUR INVESTMENTS

Sprinkler Installations

Our professionals will install a sprinkler system that fits your businesses needs and requirements. Sprinkler systems protect businesses and homes from potential fire damage. Sprinkler systems are important to protecting your business and assets.

Alarm Installations

Our professionals will install a Fire or Security alarm for you business or home. Fire alarms systems alert people and the proper authorities about fires and fire related issues. Security systems prevent potential break-ins and give you peace if mind about your assets.

Alarm Inspections

Our professionals will inspect your Security and Fire alarms to make sure they are working as expected. A faulty alarm system could prevent it from protecting your business if a break in or damages would occur.

Sprinkler Inspections

Our professionals will inspect your businesses sprinkler system to make sure it is up to code. We will make sure your sprinkler system is working to protect your business from fires.

Fire Extinguishers

We will supply and refill fire extinguishers for your home or business. Fire extinguishers will protect your business and home from fires.

Need work?

Does your business need an alarm system or sprinkler system? Or just an inspection for one of your systems? No problem! Feel free to contact us and we will get back to you as soon as possible.
