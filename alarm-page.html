ALARM SYSTEMS

Why You Need Them

We install two types of alarm systems; fire alarms and security alarms. Both are an important part of protecting business and homes.

Fire alarms are required for businesses in both Indiana and Ohio. They are an important part of keeping your employees and assets safe from potential fire risks.

Security alarms are not required in Indiana and Ohio. However, they can and will prevent burglaries and give you peace of mind about your home or business. The different types of security system we install include; cameras (indoor and outdoor), motion detectors, control panels, and more. These help prevent unwanted entry and let you keep an extra eye on your business or home.

Installations and Inspections

At Protek Corporation we will install and inspect both fire and security alarms. Our professionals install fire detection systems including smoke alarms and heat detectors. This also includes warning systems like pull stations and alarms. We inspect everything we install, so if you have an existing system we will check to see if it is still working or fix any issues with it.

We install security systems as well. Our professionals will make sure your business or home is protected. This includes cameras and detectors. If you are not sure what you want contacts us and we will help you decide what you need.

We will install and inspect any alarm system that is within 60 miles of the Richmond, Indiana area.

Why choose us?

Protek Corporation has been installing and inspecting fire alarms and security systems since 1984. We have installed systems for factories, office buildings, homes, and more. If your home or business need and alarm system, we will make sure it Proteks your assets.
